Page-level KPIs
Endpoint:
GET https://graph.facebook.com/v25.0/{page-id}/insights?metric={metric-1},{metric-2}&access_token={page-access-token}
Common metrics: page_impressions_unique, page_engaged_users, page_post_engagements, page_actions_post_reactions_total.
Requires: a Page Access Token with the analyze task, plus pages_read_engagement and read_insights permissions.
Post-level KPIs
Endpoint:
GET https://graph.facebook.com/v25.0/{page-post-id}/insights?metric={metric-1},{metric-2}&access_token={page-access-token}
Common metrics: post_impressions, post_engaged_users, post_reactions_like_total, post_reactions_love_total, post_clicks.
